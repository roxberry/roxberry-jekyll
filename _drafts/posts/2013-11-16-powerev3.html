For really no other reason than to post the equivalent of a geek selfie and to impress my kids, I coded an Excel add-in to control a Mindstorms EV3 robot.

In addition to driving the motors of the robot, I am capturing samples of near real time sensor data from it. I have a block of 50 cells used for the chart and loop through as sample data is received, creating a live chart. I hope to code more sensor data logging and move into coordinating activity from a central server.
