Running a robotics class for the past 3 years, I find reminding the students about G-d and the miracle of life fits perfectly. I added 2 laws to Isaac Asimov's</a> fundamental laws.

  1. Robot Makers are not G-d. We will never be able to create something as infinitely complex as G-d.</li>
  2. Robots are not replacements for humanity.</li>
  3. Robots we create will not injure a human being or allow a human being to be harmed.</li>
  4. Robots we create will obey our programming unless it violates law 3.</li>
  5. Robots we create will protect its own existence, as long as this does not conflict with law 3 or 4.</li>
    </ol>
